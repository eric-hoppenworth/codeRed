What we do.

  • Providing a single platform for music producers to network and colaborate with local artists.
  • Linking local performers with local venues
  • Providing a single platform that allows venue owners/promoters to audition and communicate with local artists.

How we connect you to the music.

Drop Box will allow vocalists and instrumentalists to submit their studio performances to producers, who will ultimately mix and master the stems they submit into their production.

SoundCloud – will allow performers to experience the producers productions and decide what projects they wish to be apart of producers they’d like to reach out to.
